What to Do If Your Pet Is Lost

Search Your Area

Check under porches, in garages, sheds, and bushes. Cats especially hide close to home. Search at dawn and dusk when it's quiet.

Post Everywhere

Report here, post on our Facebook, and share with Lost Dogs MN and SE-MN Lost/Found Pets.

Contact Local Vets & Shelters

Call nearby vet clinics and animal control. Someone may have brought your pet in. Ask them to scan for a microchip.

Leave Familiar Items Out

Put their bed, a worn shirt, or litter box outside your door. Familiar scents can guide them home from surprisingly far away.
