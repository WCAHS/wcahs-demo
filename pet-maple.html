About Maple

Maple is a dignified, gentle 3-year-old tabby who carries herself with the quiet confidence of a cat who knows exactly what she wants. And what she wants, more than anything, is a sunny windowsill and a calm human to sit beside. She's not the type to demand attention, but when you settle onto the couch, she'll appear beside you within minutes, purring softly.

She's incredibly low-maintenance — impeccable litter box habits, never scratches furniture, and has zero interest in counter surfing. Maple prefers a predictable routine and a peaceful environment. She does best as the only pet in the home, where she can be the quiet center of your world.

Maple's ideal home is a calm household — a single person, a couple, or a family with older children who understand that some cats prefer gentle pets over being picked up. She'd thrive in an apartment or a house, as long as there's a window with a good view.

Background

Maple was surrendered to WCAHS in May 2026 when her elderly owner of three years made the difficult decision to move into an assisted living facility that doesn't allow pets. Her owner was heartbroken and made sure Maple came with her favorite blanket, her usual brand of food, and a handwritten note about her daily routine.

It's clear Maple was deeply loved and well cared for. She arrived in excellent condition and clearly used to a structured, quiet home life. She was shy for her first day in foster care — hiding under the bed — but by day two she was rubbing against her foster parent's ankles and claiming the sunniest spot in the house.
